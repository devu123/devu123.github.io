Coffee

I've just roasted some natural-processed Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to 3 different roast levels: City, City+ and Full City. I definitely prefer City- I'm of the camp to preserve as much varietal characteristics as possible.

My second coffeeshop was a success- I served a South Indian bean, a Sumatran, a Kenyan and an El Salvadorean. And cold brewed some natural processed Ethiopian Yirgacheffe. Really remarkable conversations over the coffee- which is my favorite part, I think. This time I roasted everything I served. That was fun.

Next up is figuring out whether the moka pot should be written off from my repertoire- so little control, not my style.
